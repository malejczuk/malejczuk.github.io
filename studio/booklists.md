## American Post-War Literature

_Note: America is being (mis)used as shorthand for United States._

I came across a copy of Bukowski's Hollywood at a very sparse-shelved library in Minca, Colombia.
I joined the library, checked out the book, and devoured it in a couple of days.

It was the first English-language book I had touched in a few months.
I loved Bukowski's sentences, and I was inspired to explore more of the tradition of modern American literature.
Hence the creation of this booklist, which became my reading focus for 2025.

By no means exhaustive, the list aims to provide a fair, balanced immersion into major literary movements from the 1950s-1980s United States.
In its current form, the list may be fairly critiqued for under-sampling women and non-whites. <br><br>
