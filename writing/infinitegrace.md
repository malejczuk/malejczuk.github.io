---
layout: writing
title: Infinite Grace
date: February 14-15, 2025
---

<p>
Golden horses gallop my cherry chariot <br>
Through endless orchards of pink roses! <br>
<span class="indent-3">I am distracted by the shape of my fingernail</span> <br>
<span class="indent-3">And a song that’s stuck in my head</span> <br>
<br>
You ask me “are you blessed?” <br>
I look up and say “why yes!” <br>
<span class="indent-3">As I bask in the bliss</span> <br>
<span class="indent-3">Of the golden pink perfume</span> <br>
<br>
Ten minutes go by <br>
And I forget what I professed <br>
<span class="indent-3">Fresh petals pepper me with kisses —</span> <br>
<span class="indent-3">I feel nothing against my bare skin</span> <br>
<br>
The divine cradles me in silk blankets and I do not even notice! <br>
What will my punishment be? <br>
<br>
The price for not seeing <br>
Is not to see <br>
<span class="indent-3">And in boundless grace I am offered</span> <br>
<span class="indent-3">Another chance</span> <br>
<br>
--------------------------------------------------<br>
<br>
The humpback turtle carries me <br>
Across crystal waters to hidden caves of ancient diamonds! <br>
<span class="indent-3">	I am absorbed by a stain on my shirt</span> <br>
<span class="indent-3">	Pondering what to eat for my next meal</span> <br>
<br>
You ask me “are you blessed?” <br>
I breathe deep and say “why yes!” <br>
<span class="indent-3">As I merge with the beauty</span> <br>
<span class="indent-3">Of salty rainbows over the shimmering sea</span> <br>
<br>
Two moments slip by <br>
And I forget what I was shown <br>
<span class="indent-3">The sea serenades me sweetly —</span> <br>
<span class="indent-3">Its call mute in my deaf ears</span> <br>
<br>
The divine whispers minuets in my ear and I pay it no mind! <br>
What price will I have to pay? <br>
<br>
The cost of not hearing <br>
Is not to hear <br>
<span class="indent-3">And in boundless grace I am offered</span> <br>
<span class="indent-3">Another chance</span> <br>
<br>
--------------------------------------------------<br>
<br>
The divine’s response <br>
To forgotten blessings <br>
<span class="indent-3">Is to reveal itself</span> <br>
<span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3">Again and again</span></span> <br>
<span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3">In the pink ocean of the setting sun</span></span></span> <br>
<span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3">The fresh spring of the coconut tree</span></span></span></span> <br>
<span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3">And the hanging mist of the mountain pines</span></span></span></span></span> <br>
<br>
Our repeated ignorance <br>
<span class="indent-3">Is met with infinite grace</span> <br>
<span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3">And in boundless love we are given endless chances</span></span> <br>
<span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3">To remember</span></span></span> <br>
<span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3"><span class="indent-3">Over and over again</span></span></span></span> <br>
<br>
</p>
