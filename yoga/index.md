## My Path of Yoga
At the invitation of a friend, I began practicing yoga once per week, starting in the summer of 2019.
By the beginning of 2021, yoga had become my sole form of exercise.
I was practicing exclusively with YouTube videos.

In 2022, I joined a studio for the first time, HAUM Studios in San Francisco.
I quickly experienced large leaps in my practice.
Several months after joining, I was looking once more for a way to further deepen practice.
I signed up for the teacher training offered by my studio.

In 2023, I completed my **200-hour YTT in Hatha Vinyasa** under my teachers Danni Pomplun and Yvonne Kingsley, co-founders and lead trainers of HAUM.
The training was deeply impactful, and in its course I realized in my heart that I had to leave my job.

I left my job in early 2024 and spent one year backpacking around Latin America.
In 2025, I studied on a **monthlong Karma Yoga retreat** under my teacher Gabriel, at Yambolombia in Salento, Colombia.
Following this experience, I spent one month cradled in the Amazon jungle.

Since late 2025, I have been practicing **Mysore-style Hatha Ashtanga** under Aleix Griñó at Viveka Yoga Shala in Barcelona.
I currently teach regularly at Espai Adarsha, also in Barcelona.
